Our Identity

Curating Value in Minas Gerais

SARCA REPRESENTACOES E DESIGN LTDA (DBA: SARSA) is a premier commercial representation firm located in Belo Horizonte. While our roots are in the artistic tradition of Minas Gerais, our operation is built on the precision of securities and commodity contracts brokerage.

We treat design collections—furniture, lighting, and textiles—not merely as products, but as assets. Our role is to act as the intermediary between high-value manufacturers and the architects, developers, and retailers who shape the built environment.

Under the leadership of Alba Marcia Silva De Jesus Sampaio, we have established a reputation for reliability. We provide the market intelligence and contractual security usually reserved for financial sectors, applied to the world of aesthetic commerce.
